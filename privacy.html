1. Information We Collect

BMS may collect the following information:

  • Full names
  • Usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment-related information, including phone numbers used for payments
  • Device and usage information
  • Other information entered by users while using the application

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage user accounts and access
  • Process and record payments
  • Identify and communicate with users
  • Support administration and system management
  • Improve application performance and usability
  • Maintain records required for normal business operations

3. Storage of Information

The information collected by BMS is stored in a secure SQL database and protected using security measures intended to prevent unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties except where required by law or where necessary to operate the application securely.

5. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data, including secure storage and encryption where applicable. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. User Rights

Users may request:

  • Access to their data
  • Correction of incorrect data
  • Deletion of their data

Users may also delete their data where supported by the system or by contacting the administrator.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law or for legitimate business reasons.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact the administrator using the phone number provided in the About page of the application.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will take effect when the updated version is published in the application or on the official policy page.
